關於本站 - About Us

本站是一個基於網站前後端技術學習實踐的免費開源網站。預計順便提供一些免費且實用的小功能，短期目標是提供縮網址和圖片的上傳與瀏覽服務，中期可能視網站的完善程度將原始碼上傳GiHub做為開源專案共享。
This website is a free open-source site based on learning and practicing front-end and back-end technologies. It aims to provide some free and useful small features, with the short-term goal of offering URL shortening and image upload and browsing services. In the medium term, depending on the website's development, the source code may be uploaded to GitHub as an open-source project.

本網站營運方式：
・開發者自行搭建的Ubuntu伺服器，自掏腰包購買硬體和負擔電費與網路費等必要支出。
・本網站並無計畫導入外部廣告或提供商業加值服務，本網站將堅持提供免費服務。
・本站已盡量只使用網路上標註免費公開使用的素材，如仍有誤用或疑義請來信站方，本站會盡快更換素材。
Operating model of this website:
・A self-built Ubuntu server, with hardware purchased at our own expense and covering necessary expenses such as electricity and internet fees.
・This website has no plans to introduce external advertisements or offer commercial premium services; it will continue to provide free services.
・We strive to use materials labeled for free public use online. If there is any misuse or doubt, please contact us, and we will replace them ASAP.
